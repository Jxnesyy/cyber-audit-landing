Cyber Hygiene: Your Digital Lifeline

Neglect weak passwords and you invite disaster. Unpatched devices are ticking time-bombs. Stay alive online with these essentials:

  • Unique, Long Passwords: A single crack can expose everything.
  • Multi-Factor Authentication: Your last line of defense against credential theft.
  • Regular Patching: Hackers exploit yesterday’s holes—patch today.
  • Encrypted Backups: Ransomware can’t hold you hostage if you own the keys.
  • Security Awareness: One click on a bad link can end your digital life.
